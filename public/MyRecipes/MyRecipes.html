Spaghetti:

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Meatballs

Instructions:

  1. Place water in a deep pan over high heat until it starts to boil.
  2. While the water is boiling on a seperate skillet pan over a medium heat place your meatballs and cook thoroughly.
  3. Once the meatballs have finished cooking place the marinara sauce in the same pan with the meatballs and lower the heat to a medium setting.
  4. Once the water is boiling place your noodles in the boiling water for 12 minutes and toss in a pinch of salt.
  5. Once the noodles have finished boiling use a strainger to filter out as much water as you can and plate the noodles
  6. Pour the preferred amount of Marinara sauce w/ Meatballs onto your noodles and Enjoy!
